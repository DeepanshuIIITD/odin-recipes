How to make Lasagna chuttney at home!

Ingredients

  • crushed garlic
  • onion chopped finely
  • cooking oil
  • mustard seeds
  • red chilli powder
  • tommato
  • coriander

All done just make it now!

methods for making

  1. in a pan add 2 tsp of cooking oil wait till it heat properly
  2. add tommato,onion and mustard seed into pan
  3. keep steering it till turn pinkinsh
  4. add garlic and keep it closed for 10 min
  5. let it cool down serve with sweet beverages and dish
  6. enjoy garlic chutney
