Your First Hamburger Recipe At home!

Some important information :-

Hamburger are very easy to make it will hardly take 2-3 min of making . Which makes it best for office going people and college going students. At the same time it is very healthy and tasty. let's just make it

Ingredients

  • Burger bun
  • Onion
  • Green chilli sauce
  • Tomamato
  • Chaat Masala!
  • Cauliflower
  • Cheese
  • Italian Mayonnaise

Steps to make it

  1. cut slice a bun in two halfs
  2. put onion and tommato slice dipped in butter on one side
  3. pour some green sauce and Some grained cauliflower on other side
  4. now put some Mayonnaise and Cheese with some Chaat Masala
  5. gently close the bun and put it in microwave for 2 min
  6. You are done serve it, with some ketchup

Enjoy your burger
